  • How much experience do you have?

  • Are the pricing plans fixed?

    I offer a base package for $250, suitable for most small businesses. I quote more complex projects on a case-by-case basis.

    For more details, see the Services Page.

  • Why should I get a website?

    A website is essential for any business owner who wants to grow their business. If you still need to get a website, invest in one. It's one of the best things you can do for your business.

    Here are a few things it can help you do:

    • Establish Credibility
    • Reach a Wider Audience
    • Generate Leads and Sales
    • Provide Customer Service
    • Build a Brand
    • Stay Up-to-Date

    For more details, see the section on why to invest in a website.

  • How fast can you deliver?

    The duration of the entire process from start to finish depends on several factors. For a ballpark, expect a finished basic marketing site in 2-3 weeks. Sometimes, it can be much quicker.

  • What does the process look like?

    There are several steps to the process.

    1. We start by discussing your needs and how I can help.
    2. We will sign a contract to protect both of us.
    3. I will send an invoice consistent with the payment structure in the contract - generally 50% of the total price.
    4. We will have an in-depth discussion of how your business works and what you need from a website.
    5. I’ll present a design and request any needed assets or additional information.
    6. I will code the website and provide you with a demo link.
    7. Upon final approval, I will send an invoice for the remaining amount. If hosting is required, I will also provide a monthly recurring invoice for you to pay.
    8. The website will go live, and customers will start pouring in!

    For more details, see the Process Page.
