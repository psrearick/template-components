Description of Services

  • Basic Marketing Website

    This type of website can have up to 5 pages. It aims to present your services and tell people about yourself. Five pages are plenty to appear professional and competent while providing all the information the visitor needs. It is essential to keep your website concise and easy to navigate. Too many pages can make it difficult for visitors to find the information they seek. Here are some standard pages marketing websites have:

    • Homepage: The homepage is the most important page on your website, as it is the first page that most visitors will see. It should give visitors a clear and concise overview of your business, its products or services, and its contact information.
    • About page: The About page is a great place to tell your business's story and introduce your team to visitors. You can also use this page to highlight your company's mission, values, and unique selling proposition (USP).
    • Products or services page: This page should list all the products or services that your business offers. Be sure to include clear and concise descriptions of each product or service and high-quality images or videos.
    • Contact page: The contact page is where visitors can contact your business. Be sure to include your contact information, such as your phone number, email address, and mailing address.
    • In addition to these essential pages, websites often include a portfolio page, a testimonials page, or a FAQ page.

    A few features I do not include in this service are sometimes helpful, such as a blog, a contact form, or a place for visitors to leave reviews. These features can be beneficial but are optional for most small businesses. For this reason, if these or other complex features are essential to your company, I can provide a customized quote for your business needs.

  • Content Creation

    Sometimes, clients who want a website know what they wish each page to say. Sometimes they don’t. I offer content creation services when they do not know what to say.

    Content creation is a general term that means developing the words and images that appear on a website. Copywriting is a big part of content creation, writing the words on each page.

    Copywriting is an essential aspect of web development. It is the process of generating informative and engaging content for a website. Copywriting includes writing persuasive text that encourages visitors to take a desired action, such as signing up for a newsletter or purchasing.

    Content creation serves several purposes in the web development process:

    • To educate and inform visitors. Website content should give visitors the information they need to learn about a business, its products or services, and its industry.
    • To build trust and credibility. Well-written website content can help build trust and credibility with visitors, which is vital because visitors are likelier to do business with a business they trust.
    • To persuade visitors to take action. Website copy should be persuasive and encourage visitors to take a desired action, such as signing up for a newsletter, purchasing products, or contacting the business.
    • To improve search engine optimization (SEO). Search engines like Google use website content to determine how relevant a website is to a search query. Copywriters can improve the website's SEO ranking by creating high-quality, informative content and attracting more visitors, increasing the website’s visibility.

    Before a developer writes any code for a website, a copywriter should create the site's content, which will help ensure that the content is relevant, informative, and persuasive and that the site's design is well suited to its content.

    Here are some specific examples of how web designers use content creation in the web development process:

    • Homepage: The homepage is the most crucial page on a website. I carefully craft it to educate and inform visitors about the business and its offerings. The homepage should also include persuasive copy encouraging visitors to take a desired action.
    • Product or service pages: Product or service pages should provide visitors with detailed information about the business's products or services. The copy on these pages should be clear, concise, and persuasive, highlighting the benefits of the products or services to the customer.
    • Blog: A blog is a great way to keep a website fresh and engaging with new content regularly. A copywriter can use blog posts to educate and inform visitors about the business, its industry, and other relevant topics. Blog posts can promote the business's products or services and encourage visitors to act. Note that while I sometimes create websites with blogs, I do not provide copywriting services for blog posts.
    • Call to action (CTA): A CTA is a text or a button that encourages visitors to take a desired action. A designer places throughout the website, and they should be persuasive and direct.

    Using content creation and copywriting effectively, I can create informative, engaging, and persuasive websites.

  • Web Hosting

    Web hosting is a service that provides storage space and bandwidth for websites. It is where your website's files are stored so that visitors can access them.

    Domain registration is registering a unique domain name for your website. People will type your domain name into their web browser to visit your website. For example, I own the domain prwebconsulting.com.

    To create a website, you need both web hosting and domain registration. You can register your domain name through a domain registrar and purchase web hosting from a web hosting provider. Many web hosting providers also offer domain registration services.

    Email accounts can use your domain name. For example, an account name “phil” on the domain “prwebconsulting.com” would be phil@prwebconsulting.com. Email accounts with your domain appear much more professional than email accounts on a public email service, such as those ending in @google.com. Some domain registrars and web hosting providers offer email services as well.

    Once you have registered a domain name and purchased a web hosting plan, you can upload your website's files to your web hosting provider's server. Once your website's files are uploaded, you can visit your website by typing your domain name into a web browser.

    Web hosting and domain registration are essential services for anyone who wants to create a website. By choosing the right web hosting plan and domain name, you can create a reliable, fast, and easy-to-find website.
